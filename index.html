App Features

Four tools, one app.

Everything you need to convert, listen, and communicate — clean, fast, and completely free.

01
Speech to Text

Record your voice and watch it convert to text instantly. Perfect for notes, dictation, and captions. Simply speak — the app handles the rest. Copy, paste, or share the result in seconds.

02
Text to Speech

Turn any written content into natural spoken audio. Supports written text, PDFs, eBooks, websites, and office documents — read aloud in a natural tone across multiple languages.

03
Translate

Translate text into any language with just a few taps. Choose the file or type your text, pick your target language, and get an instant translation. Save it or email it directly to your contacts.

04
Speech Notes & History

Speak to record notes — the app instantly converts audio to text as your voice is detected. All your sessions are saved so you can go back, copy text, or share with contacts at any time.
